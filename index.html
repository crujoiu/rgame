How to play

Tap the game area or press Space to jump over incoming obstacles. Trigger a super jump with a quick double-tap or two fast Space presses.

The run gets faster as your score increases, so focus on rhythm and timing. Use short jumps for low obstacles and save super jumps for tighter patterns.
